Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

At NutriScan, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best experience, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Camera and Photos: We require access to your device's camera and photo library to allow you to scan food items and barcodes for nutritional tracking. Images are processed to identify food and are not permanently stored on our servers without your explicit permission.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data on how you interact with the app to help us improve performance and fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To analyze food images and provide nutritional information.
  • To track your daily progress towards your health goals.
  • To improve the accuracy of our AI recognition models.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your personal health profile is stored locally on your device or securely on encrypted servers. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

NutriScan may use third-party services for analytics or crash reporting. These services may collect information sent by your device as part of a standard request.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
