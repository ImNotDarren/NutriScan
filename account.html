Delete Account

We are sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below within the NutriScan mobile application.

1
Open the Menu Tap the menu icon (three lines) in the top left corner of the screen.
2
Go to Your Account At the bottom of the menu, tap the button with your email address or your name.
3
Initiate Account Deletion Tap "Delete Account" and confirm your choice in the pop-up prompt.

Important Data Policy

7-Day Grace Period: Once you initiate the deletion, your account enters a "pending deletion" state. You have 7 days to cancel this request by simply logging back into the app.

After 7 days, your account, history, logs, and all personal data will be permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.
